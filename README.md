# Canadian Citizenship Test Practice

Practice for the Canadian citizenship exam. **[→ Open app](https://mattfung99.github.io/ca-citizenship-test/)**

Two modes: a timed real test (20 questions, 45 min, pass at 15/20) or open-ended practice with configurable length and feedback. 600 questions drawn from the
[Richmond Public Library answer keys](https://www.yourlibrary.ca/citizenship-test-answer-keys/) and [mycitizen.ca](https://www.mycitizen.ca/full-simulation-tests.html), with additional questions created directly from the official [_Discover Canada_](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/corporate/publications-manuals/discover-canada/read-online/) study guide chapters.

Sign in to sync your history across devices. No account needed as results are saved in the browser for guests.
